How Tabang Hub Works

Our platform combines donations, secondhand shopping, and fundraising to create a sustainable ecosystem of support.

Direct Donations

Support registered charities across Cebu with money, food, clothes, and essential items.

Marketplace

Browse and purchase affordable pre-loved items while supporting sustainable living.

Sell & Donate

Sell your used or unused items and choose which charity you'd like to support.

Reduce Waste

Shop & Give Back

Discover affordable used and unused items from community members. Every donation and purchase can make a difference. When an item is sold, the funds go directly to the seller's chosen charity, helping support communities across Cebu while promoting sustainable and eco-friendly living.
